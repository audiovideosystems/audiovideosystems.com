outdoor entertainment

Today's home entertainment experience is no longer confined within a home's four walls. Here are some ways AVS brings music and movies outdoors.

Sonance Landscape Speakers

Experts in environmental audio reproduction, Sonance produces a perfectly balanced solution for outdoor audio with their Landscape Series line. High quality speakers and buried subwoofers are strategically located around the perimeter of any outdoor listening space to deliver immersive, high quality music. Driven by DSP amplifiers, these loudspeakers are perfect for just about any environment.

Séura Outdoor TVs

Séura is an innovative company that specialized in the blend of technology and décor, and their leading-edge HDTV for outdoor applications is their Séura Storm. Capable of withstanding the elements, and providing a bright and vibrant image even in direct sunlight, the Storm series allows the user to enjoy high definition video in the great outdoors.

Coastal Source Lighting

In addition to music and video, the outdoor entertainment experience can be enhanced by lighting. Coastal Source has developed a state-of-the-art landscape lighting system. Their elegant and durable brass fixtures will last a lifetime, and they are the only landscape lighting company to use LED instead of incandescent bulbs for energy efficiency.

AVS has firsthand knowledge of the build quality of Coastal Source Lighting’s system. A client of ours with a waterfront home was virtually wiped out by Superstorm Sandy, yet his Coastal Source lighting system survived.
